About Me

Ha Phan is a Senior Manager in CSC Corptax Professional Services group.  Ha has been with the company since 2009.  She has been involved in multiple implementation projects utilizing wide range of Corptax products such as Corptax Provision, Tax Contingency (formerly FIN48), and Corptax Office. Ha graduated from Manchester University, Indiana with bachelor’s degrees in Accounting and Finance.  She is also a graduate of Kellstadt Graduate School of Business - DePaul University, Illinois with a Master of Accountancy.  Ha iscurrently a Certified Corptax Professional in Provision and a Registered CPA inthe state of Illinois.
